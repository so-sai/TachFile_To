# MISSION 2026-007: Violation Reporting System

**Mission ID:** 2026-007  
**Status:** PLANNING (Pivot from Enforcement)  
**Created:** 2026-01-30  
**Owner:** Architect  

---

## 1. DECISION CONTEXT (PIVOT)

### Background
Per **MDS Alignment V1.2**, we are moving away from "Contractual Enforcement" (where TachFileTo has the power to block data) towards a "Violation Reporting System" (where TachFileTo detects discrepancies and reports them to `iron_core`).

### Problem Statement
TachFileTo should not have the authority to make final decisions on data validity. It only acts as a **Truth Shaper**. The **Truth Holder** (`iron_core`) must receive clear, structured reports to make the final judgment.

### Strategic Goal
Establish a robust "Detect & Declare" pipeline that identifies violations (e.g., schema mismatch, abnormal Veto-rate > 20%, data corruption) and signs them as **Pre-Truth Artefacts**.

---

## 2. SCOPE

### In-Scope
1. **Reporting Logic**
   - Detection of internal rule violations (SOP breaches).
   - Structured JSON reporting of "Violations" vs "Enforcement requests".
   - Aggregation of "Veto-rate" metrics per ingestion task.

2. **Integration with iron_core**
   - New reporting schema in `INGESTION_SCHEMA.json` (or side-car JSON).
   - "Violation Artefact" generation.

3. **Janitor Cleanup**
   - Ensuring all reporting artefacts are ephemeral and cleaned by the Janitor.

## 4. SUCCESS CRITERIA

1. **Separation of Powers:** TachFileTo can identify a violation but **cannot** stop the flow without `iron_core` approval.
2. **Deterministic Reporting:** Every violation is logged with a cryptographic signature and a clear reason code.
3. **Ghost-Free:** No violation reports persist after the task is finished/acknowledged.
