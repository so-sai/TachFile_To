- **Role:** Central Business Logic / Ledger / Validation Engine
- **Status:** `CENTRAL_LOGIC ‚Äî RESTRICTED`

### 2. Rules of Engagement
1.  **No Implicit Access:** `TachFileTo` must not import, reference, or replicate `iron_core` logic without an explicit Interface Contract (e.g., `core_contract` module).
2.  **Data Sovereignty:** Data generated by `TachFileTo` is "Raw/Unverified" until accepted by `iron_core`.
3.  **Logic Separation:**

    -   `iron_core`: Valuation, Diff, Ledger, Final Judgment.
